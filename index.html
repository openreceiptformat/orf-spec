OpenRF

Building the Open Standard for Digital Receipts & Menus.

One Schema. Every Device. Total Privacy.

OpenRF is an open protocol designed to replace paper waste with secure, local-first digital assets.

Receipta

The User Vault. An open-source mobile app for consumers to collect and manage itemized receipts without ever sharing an email address.

Tapir (Powered by Tommy)

The Merchant Platform. A "Tap-Here" infrastructure for stores to provide menus and instantly mint receipts over NFC.

The Protocol (OpenRF v1.0)

  "orf_version": "1.0",

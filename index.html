OpenRF

Standardizing Digital Receipts & Menus

One Protocol. Unlimited Potential.

A privacy-first, open standard for digital receipts

250 billion paper receipts are printed annually.

They're toxic, easily lost, and impossible to organize.

ORF is the open alternative.

Why ORF?

For Users

  • Own your data (stored locally, not in the cloud)
  • Works with any app that supports ORF
  • Never lose a receipt again

For Merchants

  • Reduce paper costs ($500-2000/year)
  • Enable digital loyalty programs
  • Better customer engagement

For Developers

  • One format, infinite apps
  • Clean, structured data
  • No vendor lock-in
